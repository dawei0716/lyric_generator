Data Set used:
Corpus of lyrics of all songs from: "Kendrick Lamar" (2009),"Section.80" (2011), "Good Kid: M.A.A.D City" (2012), "To Pimp A Butterfly" (2015), "untitled unmastered." (2016), "DAMN." (2017), "Black Panther" (2018)

Model used:
Recurrent Neural Network using Tensorflow

What is "Temperature"?:
It is a hyperparameter. Lower temperatures generate more predictable text (resembles closer to actual corpus).
Higher temperatures generate more creative text, but at the cost of more mistakes such as spelling mistake.
